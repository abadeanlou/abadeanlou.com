⚠ This data is more than 30 minutes old — the publisher may be down. The pipeline reports its own staleness rather than hiding it.

Every minute, a collector on a GCP VM polls the public GBFS feeds of BikeMi (Milan) and Vélib' (Paris) into partitioned Parquet. Apache Airflow compacts each finished day, backs everything up to Cloud Storage, watches freshness, trains nightly forecasts, rebuilds the dbt + DuckDB analytics marts, and publishes this page's data every 5 minutes. Built by Amirhesam Badeanlou as a fully open, end-to-end data engineering project.
