⚠ This data is more than 2 hours old — the publisher may be down. The pipeline reports its own staleness rather than hiding it.

Every 3 minutes, a collector on a GCP VM polls the public GBFS feeds of BikeMi (Milan) and Vélib' (Paris) into partitioned Parquet. Apache Airflow compacts each finished day, backs everything up to Cloud Storage, watches freshness, and publishes this page's data hourly. Built by Amirhesam Badeanlou as a fully open, end-to-end data engineering project.
